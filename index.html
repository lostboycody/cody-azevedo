BIOGRAPHY

I am lostboycody, some people also know me as Cody Azevedo. I'm a junior Software and Web Developer based in California, knowledgable to all things Disneyland, running, and Australian psych rock n' roll.

When I'm not at Disneyland or on a skateboard, I tend to tell computers to do things through languages such as Python, Java, and JavaScript. I also sometimes tell web browsers how newly created websites should look and what they should display. I like to think I have a keen eye for design through years of personal experience and can make anything you throw at me look superb.

With moderate experience in front-end web development, including HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript, I've created websites for fun and tested my knowledge by simulating algorithms/games through 3D-graphic JS libraries. Having experimented in a wide range of fields, from Android development with Kivy libraries, to UI frameworks such as PyQt, to implementing encryption algorithms; I've proven able to quickly advance my grasp on any form of development.

My current passion project is Darkscrawl, a minimal yet fully-featured emacs-esque text editor written entirely in PyQt. If you're interested in the old-school programming experience of Emacs and are intimidated by its complicated usage, feel free to give it a try!
